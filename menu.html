About Sultan Naga Dimaporo

Sultan Naga Dimaporo is a first-class municipality in the province of Lanao del Norte, Philippines. It is located in the western part of the province, along the coast of Illana Bay. The municipality serves as a major agricultural center in the region.

Formerly known as Karomatan, the municipality was renamed Sultan Naga Dimaporo in 1956 after a prominent Maranao leader and politician. The area has a rich cultural heritage influenced by Maranao traditions and Islamic practices.

Sultan Naga Dimaporo is known for its agricultural economy, with rice, corn, coconut, and various fruits as major crops. The municipality also has significant fishing activities along its coastal areas.

With a diverse population, Sultan Naga Dimaporo celebrates its cultural heritage through various festivals and events throughout the year, showcasing the municipality's arts, traditions, and agricultural products.

The municipality is led by the local government unit headed by the Municipal Mayor, along with the Vice Mayor and members of the Sangguniang Bayan (Municipal Council).

Municipal Services

Business Permits and Licensing

The Municipality of Sultan Naga Dimaporo provides efficient processing of business permits, licenses, and other related documents. Our Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO) is committed to providing quality service to all business owners in the municipality.

Business Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Civil Registry

The Municipal Civil Registry Office manages and records vital statistics including births, deaths, marriages, and other civil registry documents. We ensure that all records are well-maintained and accessible to citizens when needed.

Available Services: Birth Certificate, Marriage Certificate, Death Certificate, CENOMAR, and other civil registry documents.

Health Services

The Municipal Health Office provides comprehensive health services to residents, including medical consultations, maternal and child health care, immunization, and nutrition programs. Our team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to ensuring the well-being of the entire community.

Regular Health Programs: Immunization, Maternal Care, Dental Services, Nutrition Programs, and more.

Agriculture Support

The Municipal Agriculture Office provides technical assistance, resources, and support to farmers and agricultural workers. We offer various programs aimed at improving agricultural productivity, sustainability, and income generation.

Programs: Farm Input Subsidies, Technical Training, Market Linkages, and Crop Insurance Assistance.

Social Welfare Services

The Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office provides various social welfare programs and services to vulnerable sectors including children, elderly, persons with disabilities, and families in need. We aim to improve the quality of life of our constituents through our support programs.

Services: Financial Assistance, Relief Operations, Community Development, and Livelihood Programs.

Education Support

The municipality actively supports educational initiatives through scholarships, school facilities improvement, and educational assistance programs. We believe that education is key to the development of our community.

Programs: Municipal Scholarship Program, School Supplies Distribution, and Educational Infrastructure Support.
